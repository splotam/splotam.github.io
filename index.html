The SPLOTAM story and a special soundboard

In webisode 634, the crew was discussing how it feels to be older and out of touch with trendy social apps. RMC attempted to come up with some names of fictitious social media apps, but R-Type eventually coined "splotam" and the rest of the crew added to the idea throughout the episode. RMC even mentioned they should create the app. I have selected a few audio clips below:

"It's got some annoying guy saying it everytime, like SPLOTAM!"

"No matter how you have your phone set... silence, do not disturb, or anything, it overrides it"

"Hey bro, we wanna make sure you get your SPLOTAM!"
